MSc Research Project

Fluorescent Labelling of Insulin

Fluorescent Labelling of Insulin (Actrapid) with Eosin Isothiocyanate
De Montfort University, Leicester  ·  Sep 2023 – Sep 2024  ·  Individual Project  ·  Outcome: MERIT

Fluorescently labelled insulin enables pharmacokinetic and receptor-binding studies without radioactive tracers. The challenge: producing a mono-labelled conjugate — exactly one fluorescent dye at one site — to preserve biological activity. EITC was chosen over FITC for superior photostability. The project synthesised, purified, characterised, and assessed stability under varied conditions using RP-HPLC (Shimadzu LabSolutions), ATR-FTIR (Bruker), and UV-Vis spectroscopy.
